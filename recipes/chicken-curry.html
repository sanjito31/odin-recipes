Chicken Curry

Description

Succulent chunks of chicken simmered in a reduction of aromatic spices, onions, and tomatoes

Ingredients

  • Chicken Breast
  • Red Onions
  • Tomatoes
  • Oil
  • Cumin Seeds
  • Cloves
  • Fennel Seeds
  • Cinamon Sticks
  • Turmeric Powder
  • Corriander Powder
  • Red Chili Powder
  • Salt
  • Ginger Garlic Paste
  • Thai chili peppers
  • Fresh cilantro

Steps

  1. Saute fennel seeds, cumin seeds, cloves, and cinamon sticks in oil until slighly fragrant.
  2. Add diced onions and sliced thai chili peppers. Saute until golden
  3. Add a pinch of Turmeric powder. Saute for 1 minute
  4. Reduce heat and add 3 spoons ginger garlic paste. Add 1-2 tablespoons of water. Fry until fragrant, approx 2-3 minutes
  5. Add 2 spoons corriander, 1 spoon red chili powder, 1 tablespoons salt. Mix well under low heat
  6. Increase heat back to medium-low, add diced tomatoes, mix, and cover for 5 minutes
  7. Once tomatoes break down and release water, add chicken, and cover for additional 3-5 minutes
  8. Stir mixture well, add 3/4 cup of water, mix again, bring to boil, then reduce to simmer and cover for 20 minutes
  9. Stir occassionally and cook until desired consistency.
  10. Once done, turn off heat, stir in chopped cilantro, and cover for a minute or two
  11. Garnish with more cilantro and serve
