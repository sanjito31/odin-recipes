2X Spicy Buldak Noodles

Description

2X spicy buldak noodles from Samyang Company, Korea. Very spicy and tasty noodles. Thicker and chewier than ramen noodles. These ones are more stir fry style

Ingredients

  • noodle package
  • water

Steps

  1. Boil water
  2. Add noodles
  3. After 3-4 minutes, drain noodles except like 2 tablepoons worth of water
  4. Add buldak sauce packet and mix over residual heat
  5. Transfer to bowl and add furikake
