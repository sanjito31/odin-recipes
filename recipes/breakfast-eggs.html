Breakfast Eggs

Description

These eggs are something of my own creation that my mom used to make. It's pretty much regular eggs but cooked pretty well. It's also spicy with indian ingredients

Ingredients

  • Eggs
  • Ginger garlic paste
  • Salt
  • Red chili powder
  • Thai chili peppers

Steps

  1. Saute sliced chili peppers on med-low heat in some oil
  2. In the meantime, beat two eggs in a bowl, then add ginger garlic paste, salt, and red chili powder, and mix well
  3. Add egg mixture to pan
  4. Mix in pan to get small curds to form. Fold over and cook until nothing runs
  5. Serve and enjoy
